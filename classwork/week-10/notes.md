### prompt structure

- [SAY ONCE] I am building ___
- I need help with ___
- My problem is __
- Can you explain how to fix this:
    [PASTE CODE]
- or ask AI what code it needs to see

## good prompts
- explain __ in simple terms, give 3 example
- add JS to this button, make it console.log hello when clicked
- show me how to change the background color of a page
- give me 3 different hover ideas for my button
- my code, is not working can you explain why and how to fix: [PASTE CODE]
